How We Calculate Your Score

Step 1
Text Preprocessing

We clean and normalize both your resume and the job description, removing noise and standardizing the format.

Step 2
TF-IDF Vectorization

Convert text documents into numerical vectors that represent the importance of each word and phrase.

Step 3
Cosine Similarity

Calculate the cosine of the angle between document vectors to measure content similarity (0-1 scale).

Step 4
Skill Bonus

Add bonus points for each matched skill to reward specific keyword alignment with job requirements.

Final Score Formula:
Final Score = min((Cosine Similarity × 100) + (Matched Skills × 2), 100)
The final score is capped at 100% for the best possible match.
